NEET

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is conducted for admission to medical and dental courses across India. It includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination)

JEE is conducted for admission to various undergraduate engineering programs in India. It consists of two exams - JEE Main and JEE Advanced.

BPSC (Bihar Public Service Commission)

BPSC exams are conducted for various administrative positions in the Bihar state government. It includes subjects like General Studies, History, and Political Science.

SSC (Staff Selection Commission)

SSC conducts exams for recruitment to various government departments and offices in India. It includes exams like SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, and others.

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission)

UPSC conducts civil service exams for recruitment to administrative positions in the Indian government. It includes subjects like History, Geography, and General Studies.
